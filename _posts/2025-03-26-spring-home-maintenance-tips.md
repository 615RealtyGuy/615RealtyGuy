*Protect Your Investment with These Essential Spring Maintenance Tips*

As the chill of winter melts away and the days grow longer, spring brings a perfect opportunity to give your home the care and attention it needs. Whether you're planning to sell, just moved in, or you're settling in for the long haul, seasonal maintenance not only protects your investment but can also boost curb appeal and home value.

Here are some essential spring home maintenance tips to help you start the season strong:

## 1. Inspect the Roof and Gutters

Winter can be tough on your roof. Check for missing or damaged shingles, signs of water intrusion, or debris buildup in your gutters. Clean out leaves and twigs to make sure your gutters are flowing freely — this prevents water damage to your foundation and siding.

## 2. Service Your HVAC System

Before the summer heat hits, make sure your air conditioning system is in top shape. Schedule a professional inspection and replace your air filters. A well-maintained HVAC system runs more efficiently, saving you money on energy bills.

## 3. Refresh Landscaping and Outdoor Spaces

Trim back overgrown bushes, remove dead plants, and consider adding mulch to flower beds for a fresh look. Clean patios, decks, and outdoor furniture so you're ready to enjoy the warmer weather. If you're planning to sell, fresh landscaping is one of the quickest ways to improve curb appeal.

## 4. Check for Exterior Damage

Inspect siding, brick, and foundation for any cracks or signs of damage. Look for peeling paint or caulk that needs replacing. A little touch-up now can prevent bigger repairs down the line.

## 5. Clean Windows and Screens

Winter grime builds up fast! Give your windows a good wash — inside and out — and repair or replace damaged screens. Clean windows let in more light and make your home feel brighter and more inviting.

## 6. Test Sprinklers and Outdoor Faucets

Turn on your irrigation system and outdoor water sources to check for leaks or pressure issues. Early spring is the perfect time to catch and repair problems before your lawn or garden needs consistent watering.

## 7. Declutter and Deep Clean Inside

Spring is the perfect time for a refresh inside your home too. Clean out closets, scrub baseboards, shampoo carpets, and open windows to air things out. If you're preparing to list your home, a sparkling clean interior goes a long way with buyers.
