A composite index of Nashville metro housing and economic indicators
? How is this calculated?

The 615 Score combines active inventory, days on market, home price appreciation, unemployment, income growth, and inflation into a single market health index. Each metric is normalized against its trailing 24-month range and weighted by importance. Updated as new data is released.

615 Score is a proprietary index calculated by SixOneFive Real Estate Advisors using Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED).
