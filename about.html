About Me

Before stepping into real estate, I dedicated my career to serving others as an ICU nurse. In that role, I learned the importance of precision, care, and unwavering commitment — qualities I now bring to every real estate transaction.

Buying or selling a home is one of the most significant decisions a person can make, and just like in healthcare, every situation is unique and requires a personalized approach. I take my role as your real estate professional seriously, providing the best resources, market insights, and guidance to help you make informed decisions with confidence.

My mission is simple: to help people. Whether it was in the ICU or now in real estate, my goal has always been to offer support, expertise, and peace of mind when it matters most.

With my background in high-pressure, detail-oriented work, you can trust that I will handle your real estate needs with the same level of dedication and care.
