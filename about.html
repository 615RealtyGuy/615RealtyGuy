About Me

Before stepping into the world of real estate, I dedicated my career to serving others as an ICU nurse. In that role, I learned the importance of precision, care, and unwavering commitment—qualities that I now bring to my work in real estate.

Buying or selling a home is one of the most significant decisions a person can make, and just like in healthcare, every situation is unique and requires a personalized approach. I take my role as your real estate professional seriously, providing you with the best resources, market insights, and guidance to make informed decisions with confidence.

My mission is simple: to help people. Whether it was in the ICU or now in real estate, my goal has always been to offer support, expertise, and peace of mind when it matters most. I strive to make every transaction as smooth and stress-free as possible, ensuring my clients feel empowered every step of the way.

With my background in high-pressure, detail-oriented work, you can trust that I will handle your real estate needs with the same level of dedication and care.

Let’s work together to achieve your real estate goals.
